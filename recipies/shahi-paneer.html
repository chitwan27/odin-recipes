Shahi Paneer

Description:

Shahi Paneer is a dish that proves true to its name.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer
  • Ground Spices
  • Onions
  • Tomato Purree

Steps:

  1. Add some Tomato Purree, salt, cut onions, and spices into a pan.
  2. Cook until the consistency is ketchup like.
  3. Add diced paneer pieces into the curry and cook for 5 minutes.
