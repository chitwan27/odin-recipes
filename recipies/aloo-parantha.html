Aloo Parantha

Description:

Aloo Parantha or as I call it "aloo di paronthi" is one my favourite dishes ever.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Bread Dough
  • Ground Spices
  • Onions

Steps:

  1. Boil some patatoes, then peel and mash them.
  2. Add some cut onions, spices and salt into them mash.
  3. Break the dough into small pieces, and make small flat balls.
  4. Mix the mashed potatoes and fill some into each ball of dough.
  5. Make sure the filling is not exposed and roll the balls into disks.
  6. Heat a pan with some grease, lay the disks flat and roast until golden brown.
