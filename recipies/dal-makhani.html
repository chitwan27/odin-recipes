Dal Makhani

Description:

Dal Makhani is one the staple of the North Indian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Urad Dal
  • Ground Spices
  • Butter
  • Onions

Steps:

  1. Wash some Urad Dal and soak it overnight.
  2. Add the Dal and four times as much water into a pot.
  3. Cook the above for about an hour, or until the consistency is amicable,
  4. In a frying pan heat some oil, add cut onions, spices and salt.
  5. When the onions are browned, add the cooked Dal into the pan.
  6. Cook until everything is throroughly mixed, serve topped with butter.
