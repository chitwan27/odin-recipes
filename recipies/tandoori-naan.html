Tandoori Naan

Description:

Tandoori Naan shows us how a humble plant such as wheat can be elevated to the glory of the highest order.

Ingredients:

  • Refined Flour
  • Ghee

Steps:

  1. Make a dough with the flour and add some ghee and salt into it.
  2. Break the dough into small pieces and turn them into balls.
  3. Roll the balls into cylindrical disks, and preheat the oven.
  4. Apply some grease to a pan, place the pan in the oven.
  5. After two minutes filp the disk and cook until golden brown.
